Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how "Unpredictable Runner" ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data. The "Unpredictable Runner" application functions entirely locally on your device.

2. Permissions

The app requires minimal permissions to function (e.g., Internet access for loading ads if applicable, or basic game functionality). We do not access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

3. Local Storage

Game progress (high scores) is saved locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external server.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, you can contact us via our GitHub repository.
