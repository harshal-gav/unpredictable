Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb 20, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Unpredictable Runner" Chrome Extension and Web Game. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Our extension acts simply as a shortcut to launch the "Unpredictable Runner" web game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data through the Chrome Extension itself.

Third-Party Services (Web Game)

When you click the extension to play the game on the web, you are directed to our Firebase-hosted site. That site utilizes:

  • Google Analytics: To measure web traffic and game engagement.
  • Google AdSense: To serve advertisements when a player crashes.

These third-party services may use cookies and collect data as outlined in Google's privacy policies. The Chrome Extension itself does NOT inject ads or read your browsing history.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via GitHub.
